To add pre-encoded content:
    -Connect to streaming server
    -In finder go to /Macintosh HD/Library/WowzaMediaServer-3.6.3/WowzaMediaServer/content/
    -Any file placed here will be accessible by connecting to rtsp://164.76.124.33:1935/vod/mp4:<filename>.mp4/

To add a new webpage:
    -Connect to webserver
    -In finder go to /Macintosh HD/Library/Webserver/Documents/
    -Any file placed here will be accessible by connecting to 164.76.124.68/<file directory>
    -Current livestream pages are under /Macintosh HD/Library/Webserver/Documents/live
    -Current video on demand pages are under /Macintosh HD/Library/Webserver/Documents/vod
